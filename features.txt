Features

📍 Map showing nearby gas stations

💰 Real-time price comparison

📊 Weekly price trend prediction

🚗 Route planner showing cheapest fuel stop

4. Fuel Impact Calculator

Problem: People don’t realize how oil price hikes affect their daily expenses.

Solution:
A tool that calculates how gas price increases affect your monthly budget.

User inputs:

Motorcycle or car

Distance per day

Fuel consumption

Output:

Monthly fuel spending

Impact of price hikes

Suggestions to save fuel

Example:

Gas price: ₱65 → ₱70

Daily commute: 20km

Result:
Monthly cost increase: ₱650
