TipidLitro turns your fuel gauge into a decision-making tool — estimate what a trip will really cost, know how far you can go before you're running on fumes, and find the nearest station without leaving the browser.

The problem

Fuel planning is mostly guesswork

Rising fuel prices hit daily commutes and business trips hard. Most motorists plan by feel, not by numbers.

  • No accurate way to estimate fuel consumption before a trip
  • No reliable way to plan cost-efficient routes
  • No accessible view of nearby fuel stations
The fix

Three tools, one dashboard

TipidLitro puts real numbers behind every trip — before you leave and while you're on the road, right in your browser, no installs.

  • Trip Calculator turns a route into a peso amount and a travel time
  • Range Calculator turns your fuel level into a kilometer figure
  • Gas Locator turns your location into a sorted list of stations
